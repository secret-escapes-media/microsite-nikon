A true urban icon, New York is a bustling metropolis that has come to define all things hip, glamorous and cool. Neon lights illuminate Times Square and Broadway in a technicolour glow, worlds apart from the hipster hangouts of Brooklyn. Diversity plays itself out in the smorgasbord of foodie treats, from Queens to to the Bronx, with galleries and renowned shopping completing the heady mix on offer.
