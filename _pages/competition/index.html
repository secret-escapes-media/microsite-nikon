Win your very own photographic adventure in Iceland

If our Iceland adventure has left your wanderlust reeling, don’t fret – it could all be yours. With two nights in Reykjavik and a further two far into the north of the country, plus a Nikon D7500 and training from Nikon, you’ll have all you need to capture your own Icelandic journey.

Fly into Reykjavik with WOW air and discover a cosmopolitan capital that easily rivals the rest of Europe’s major cities. Aside from thriving cafe and bar scene and a cultural landscape packed with artistic cool, you’ll be on the edge of the Golden Circle – the gateway to some of Iceland’s most coveted outdoor wonders. Two nights at the Icelandair Hotel Reykjavik Marina is framed by a unique, modern style throughout, with dinner at the hotel’s Slippbarinn restaurant offering a host of foodie treats.
